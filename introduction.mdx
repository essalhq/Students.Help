## Getting Started as a New Student

To ensure a smooth start at Essal Institute, we recommend completing the following steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Your Registration">
    Verify that all required administrative documents (identity card copy, birth certificate, qualifications) are submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Devices">
    Follow the [PWA Installation Guide](/technical/pwa-install) to install the portal on your phone or computer for offline access and instant notifications.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Student Portal">
    Obtain your login credentials (your @essal.dz or @essal.edu.dz email and temporary password) and complete the first-time sign-in. See [Student Portal Guide](/portal/student-guide) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tuition & Payments">
    Coordinate your tuition payment or set up installment plans via Baridimob, CCP, or at the Institute finance office.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Tips for Academic Success

* **Monitor the Portal:** Log in to [app.essal.institute](https://app.essal.institute) daily. Important announcements, exam schedules, and grades are posted exclusively on the portal.
* **Respect Attendance Limits:** Attendance is tracked electronically. Keep an eye on your status on the portal to avoid warnings or module exclusions.
* **Engage in Practical Projects:** Most IT and Business modules place a 30% weight on continuous practical projects. Start your project work early.
