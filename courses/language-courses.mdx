## Overview

Essal Language Programs are designed to enhance professional communication, prepare students for global certifications, and facilitate immigration or overseas academic enrollment. Enrolled language students gain access to interactive worksheets and mock tests on the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute).

---

## 1. English Language Programs

We offer CEFR-aligned courses from Beginner (A1) to Advanced (C1) levels.

### General English Communication
* **Focus:** Speaking confidence, pronunciation, vocabulary, and active listening.
* **Duration:** 3-month semesters (flexible evening and weekend slots).
* **Level Progression:** Each level (A1, A2, B1, B2) takes one 30-hour module to complete.

### Business English for Professionals
* **Focus:** E-writing etiquette, corporate negotiations, public presentations, and job interview preparation.
* **Duration:** 8 weeks.
* **Portal Benefit:** Digital business templates (emails, memos, resume formats) can be downloaded from the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute).

### IELTS / TOEFL Preparation Tracks
* **Focus:** Exam structure, time-management tips, mock testing sessions, and score-maximizing strategies.
* **Duration:** 10 weeks.
* **Mock Testing:** Free weekly mock tests are conducted online via the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute) under simulated exam conditions.

## 2. French Language Programs

Our French programs cater to academic and professional certification requirements.

### General French (A1 to C2)
* **Focus:** Grammar, conversational French, and reading comprehension.

### TCF / TEF Test Preparation (TCF Canada, TCF Tout Public)
* **Focus:** Practice tests for the reading, listening, writing, and speaking segments. Designed specifically for Canada immigration applicants or French university entry.
* **Duration:** 8 weeks (Intensive).
* **Validation:** Practice exams are graded by certified examiners with personalized feedback uploaded to the portal.

---

## 3. German Language Programs

Designed for students and workers aiming to relocate to Germany.

### German Levels A1, A2 & B1 (Goethe-Institut Alignment)
* **Focus:** Fundamental vocabulary, structural grammar, and everyday conversations.
* **Hours Required:**
  - **Level A1:** 80 Instruction Hours.
  - **Level A2:** 90 Instruction Hours.
  - **Level B1:** 120 Instruction Hours.
* **Credential:** Prepares students for Goethe-Zertifikat official exams.
