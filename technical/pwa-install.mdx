---

The **Essal Student Portal** is fully optimized as a Progressive Web App (PWA). This allows you to install the portal directly from your browser to your home screen or desktop without going through the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

## Why Install the PWA?

* **Instant Access:** Launch the portal directly from your home screen icon.
* **Notifications:** Receive instant push notifications for schedule changes, grades, and administrative updates.
* **Better Experience:** Run the portal in a standalone, distraction-free app window (hiding the browser URL bar and tabs).
* **Speed:** Faster page load speeds through local caching of assets.

---

## Installation on Mobile Devices

### 1. Apple iOS (iPhone & iPad)
PWAs are fully supported on iOS through the native **Safari** browser:
1. Open **Safari** on your iOS device.
2. Go to the Student Portal URL: [app.essal.institute](https://app.essal.institute).
3. Tap the **Share** button (the square icon with an arrow pointing upwards) in Safari's bottom toolbar.
4. Scroll down the share menu and select **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Set a name for the app (e.g., "Essal Student") and tap **Add** in the top-right corner.
6. The app icon will now appear on your device's home screen.

### 2. Google Android
On Android, you can install the portal using **Google Chrome** or any compatible browser:
1. Open **Google Chrome** on your Android device.
2. Navigate to [app.essal.institute](https://app.essal.institute).
3. A popup prompt reading **Add Essal to Home Screen** or **Install App** should automatically slide up from the bottom. Tap it.
4. If the popup does not appear:
   - Tap the three-dot **Menu** icon in the top-right corner of Chrome.
   - Select **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen**).
5. Confirm by tapping **Install**.
6. The app icon is now available on your home screen and in your app drawer.

---

## Installation on Desktop Computers

You can install the portal as a desktop application on **Windows, macOS, and Linux** using Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge:

1. Open **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** on your computer.
2. Navigate to the portal website: [app.essal.institute](https://app.essal.institute).
3. Look at the right side of the browser's URL address bar. You will see an **Install** icon (a monitor with a down arrow, or a plus sign).
4. Click the icon, and a popup window will ask **Install App?**.
5. Click **Install**.
6. The app will open in a standalone window, and a shortcut will be created on your desktop and applications menu.
