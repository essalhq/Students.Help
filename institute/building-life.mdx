## Building Hours

The Essal Institute building in Oran is open:
- **Saturday to Thursday:** 08:30 AM – 05:30 PM
- **Friday:** Closed

Individual class schedules (daytime, evening, or weekend blocks) are available on the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute).

---

## Computer Lab Guidelines

Our computer labs are equipped with high-performance systems and development software. To preserve our facilities, all students inside the building must adhere to the following rules:

* **Resource Preservation:** Do not modify system configurations, install unauthorized software, or tamper with networking equipment.
* **No Food or Drinks:** To protect hardware from accidental spills, food and beverages are strictly prohibited inside all computer laboratories.
* **Lab Booking:** Outside scheduled class hours, students can book workspace slots for project work by using the lab reservation tool on the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute).
* **Data Backups:** All local storage on lab machines is subject to periodic maintenance wipes. Always back up your code and projects to cloud repositories (GitHub, GitLab) or external storage drives.

## Standards of Conduct

Essal Institute is committed to fostering a professional, inclusive, and respectful learning environment inside our building.

### Student Responsibilities

* **Respect:** Respect fellow students, instructors, and administrative staff at all times.
* **Property Care:** Treat Institute property, classrooms, and lab equipment with care.
* **ID Badges:** Always carry your student ID badge while inside the Institute building. You may be asked to present it at reception or lab entrances.
* **Academic Integrity:** Plagiarism, cheating on tests, or unauthorized file sharing during assessments is strictly forbidden and subject to review by the disciplinary committee.

## Extracurricular Clubs

We encourage students to join Institute clubs to build practical projects, network, and develop professional soft skills:

* **Essal Tech Club:** Focuses on hosting hackathons, coding workshops, and open-source project development inside our building labs.
* **English Speaking Club:** Weekly meetups to practice conversational English for professional growth.
* **Entrepreneurship Hub:** A club for students looking to pitch startup ideas, refine business plans, and network with Algerian founders.

## Health & Safety Guidelines

- **First Aid:** Basic first-aid kits are available at the Institute reception desk inside the building.
- **Fire Safety:** Emergency fire exits are clearly marked in all hallways of the building. Do not obstruct these pathways.
- **Reporting Incidents:** If you experience equipment failure, safety concerns, or wish to report an incident, please visit the reception desk or submit a ticket through the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute).
