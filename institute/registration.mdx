## Registration Steps

To register for any diploma or certificate course at Essal Institute, prospective students must complete the registration form and submit the required administrative dossier.

### Required Documents Checklist

In accordance with Algerian vocational education regulations, please ensure you provide the following documents to the administrative office inside the Institute building:

* `[ ]` **National Identity Card (CNI):** A legible copy of your biometric ID card or passport.
* `[ ]` **Birth Certificate (Acte de Naissance N° 12):** One official copy.
* `[ ]` **Passport Photos:** Two (2) recent passport-sized photos with a white background.
* `[ ]` **Proof of Educational Level:** A copy of your high school diploma (Baccalauréat), university degree, or school attendance certificate (*certificat de scolarité*) depending on program entry requirements.
* `[ ]` **Registration Fee Payment Receipt:** Confirmation slip of postal/bank deposit or transfer.

## Tuition Fees and Payments

Tuition rates vary depending on the chosen track (long-term state-accredited qualifying diplomas vs. short-term certification bootcamps). Detailed fee structures are accessible on the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute).

### Payment Methods

To accommodate our students, Essal Institute supports several local payment methods:

1. **CCP (Post Office Deposit):**
   - Direct deposit into the Essal CCP account.
   - **Account Details:** Clef CCP and RIP codes are available at the Institute registrar's office.
   - You must upload a scanned copy or clear photo of the yellow postal slip (*talon*) directly to the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute) under **My Payments** and submit the physical receipt to the administration office inside the building.
2. **Baridimob Transfer:**
   - Instant mobile transfer to our official RIP account.
   - **Validation:** A screenshot of the transaction confirmation showing the date, transaction reference number, and amount must be uploaded to your account on the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute) for validation.
3. **Cash / Bank Cheque:**
   - Payments can be made directly at the finance office inside the Institute building in Oran.

---

### Payment Installments

For long-term training tracks, students are eligible for installment payment plans:
- **First Installment (40%):** Due on registration day to activate your student portal account.
- **Second Installment (30%):** Due at the mid-point of the training program (before midterm evaluation).
- **Third Installment (30%):** Due 15 days before final examinations.

<Warning>
  Failure to meet installment deadlines may result in temporary suspension of lab access in the building and [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute) services.
</Warning>

## Refund and Cancellation Policy

* **Cancellation before Course Start:** 100% of the tuition fees are refundable, minus a standard administrative registration fee.
* **Cancellation within the First 2 Weeks:** 50% of the current session tuition is refundable.
* **Cancellation after the First 2 Weeks:** No refunds can be issued for the ongoing training session.
* **How to Request a Refund:** Submit a formal refund request form through the [Student Portal](https://app.essal.institute) or at the administration office.
